About Me

IT Support Specialis

With over three years of dedicated professional experience in IT Support and IT Event Handling, I have honed my skills in troubleshooting, configuring, and resolving issues related to computers, printers, CCTV systems, and various network components such as routers, switches, and access points. Additionally, I possess extensive expertise in implementing and managing email systems for companies, utilizing platforms like Google Workspace and Cpanel. I am also proficient in executing broadcast emails through Mailchimp and efficiently collecting data from databases using MYSQL. As an IT professional, I have also excelled in Event Handling, skillfully managing ground-handling tickets and proficiently establishing and compiling schemes for ticketing exchanges, while meticulously laying out exchange areas. My commitment to delivering top-notch IT support and event-handling services has allowed me to make a significant impact in the industry and support the success of various companies.
